What time of the year are these runs?

These runs can start as early at mid July and go until late December sometime early January. It all depends on how much rainfall and water there is on the rivers to be able to have a fun and safe trip.

Do I need to be able to swim?

Yes, you must be able to swim and feel comfortable in the water. We also offer a first aid course for those who wants to work with rafting. Before the crossing, it is provided life jacket(without it, you can't enter in the boat.) It's important to note that is forbidden to take anything from nature, including rocks, plants, or animals. We preserve life. If you don't want your house destroyed, so don't destroy the house of others.

Is there a minimum age to participate?

Yes. Depending on the river and how fast the river is flowing will depend on the minimum age. Early in the season it is generally 16 yrs old. Latter in the season it wil 12 yrs old. But to guarantee your safety, it's needed a parent authorization if you are under 18 years old.

What should I wear?

For lower body: Swimming wear (or a change of underclothing) to wear under your rafting pants and tennis shoes. These will get wet.

For the upper body: Bring a long sleeved shirt to wear under your rafting jacket (it will get wet), any necessary medications (asthma inhalers, etc.) and a towel to dry off after your trip each day.

For multiple day trips make sure you bring warm cloths such as sweatshirt and pants for the evenings. It can and does get quite cold along the river. Bring a sleeping bag for overnight trips.

We supply all other equipment that you will need including the food. If you feel hungry, there is a store near the lake to you get some snacks.
